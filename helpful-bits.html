Deadlines overview

Self Assessment deadlines

  • 31 October 2025: Paper return deadline
  • 31 January 2026: Online return and payment deadline
  • 31 July 2026: Second payment on account (if applicable)

VAT deadlines

  • 1 month + 7 days after each VAT quarter end
  • Keep digital records for Making Tax Digital
  • Submit payment with return (if due)

PAYE deadlines

  • Monthly PAYE payment due by the 22nd
  • RTI submissions on or before pay day
  • Annual P60s due by 31 May
