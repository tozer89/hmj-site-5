Bookkeeping Services

Accurate, Reliable Bookkeeping for Small Businesses in the North West

At HMJ Finance, we provide bookkeeping services that keep your business organised, compliant, and ready for growth. Whether you’re a sole trader, limited company, freelancer or contractor, we make sure your financial records are up to date—so you can focus on running your business.

We work with clients across Manchester, Warrington, Liverpool, Cheshire, and the wider North West, offering straightforward, professional bookkeeping support designed around your needs.

Why good bookkeeping matters

Consistent, accurate bookkeeping keeps you compliant with HMRC and gives you the insight you need to make confident business decisions. We make sure your records are always ready for returns, funding applications, or planning your next move.
