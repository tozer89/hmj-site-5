Bookkeeping Services

Accurate, Reliable Bookkeeping for Small Businesses in the North West

At HMJ Finance, we provide bookkeeping services that keep your business organised, compliant, and ready for growth. Whether you’re a sole trader, limited company, freelancer or contractor, we make sure your financial records are up to date—so you can focus on running your business.

We work with clients across Manchester, Warrington, Liverpool, Cheshire, and the wider North West, offering straightforward, professional bookkeeping support designed around your needs.

What’s included

  • Recording income and expenses
  • Bank and credit card reconciliations
  • Monthly or quarterly reports
  • Cashbook maintenance
  • VAT return preparation (if applicable)
  • Uploading receipts and documentation to your accounting software
  • Ongoing support and catch-up bookkeeping when needed

Who we help

  • Sole traders and freelancers
  • Limited companies (including VAT-registered businesses)
  • Landlords and property companies
  • Contractors and consultants
  • Startups and growing businesses

Whether you’re looking for a hands-on bookkeeper or want to outsource everything entirely, we can help you stay organised and stress-free.

Why good bookkeeping matters

Keeping your records in order isn’t just about staying compliant—it’s about making better business decisions.

  • Clear visibility over your income and expenses
  • Accurate data for tax returns and VAT submissions
  • Fewer surprises at year-end
  • Peace of mind knowing your accounts are under control

You’ll also be better prepared for funding applications, HMRC checks, or growth opportunities.
