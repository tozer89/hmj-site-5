Who needs to file a self-assessment?

  • A sole trader or freelancer
  • A company director with income outside PAYE
  • A landlord with rental income
  • Receiving dividends or other untaxed income
  • Earning over £100,000 per year
  • Filing on behalf of a partnership
  • Claiming tax relief on pension contributions or expenses

When is the deadline?

The key deadline for online self-assessment filing is 31 January each year (covering the previous tax year ending 5 April).

  • 31 January – First payment (and any balancing payment)
  • 31 July – Second payment on account (if applicable)
