What are management accounts?

Management accounts are periodic financial reports — usually monthly or quarterly — that provide a detailed and current picture of your business’s financial health. Unlike year-end statutory accounts, management accounts are internal tools designed to help you:

  • Track revenue and expenditure against budget
  • Identify trends and cost-saving opportunities
  • Improve forecasting and financial planning
  • Prepare for funding applications or investor reviews
  • Make strategic decisions based on up-to-date data

1. Monthly or quarterly management accounts

  • Customised reporting pack delivered on an agreed schedule
  • Profit & loss reporting with variance analysis
  • Balance sheet reviews
  • Clear, jargon-free commentary and insights
  • Visual dashboards using Excel and Power BI

2. Budgeting and forecasting

  • Preparation of annual budgets
  • Rolling forecasts based on real-time figures
  • Mid-year reforecasting for stakeholders or lenders

3. Departmental or cost centre reporting

  • Allocation of costs across teams, sites, or departments
  • Individual team or depot performance tracking
  • IFRS16 lease accounting where needed

4. Strategic financial support

  • Financial reporting for board or stakeholder meetings
  • Audit and due diligence preparation
  • Internal reporting process improvements

5. Dashboard & reporting tools

  • Creation of custom Excel models
  • Real-time Power BI dashboards
  • Forecast vs actual comparisons in visual format
